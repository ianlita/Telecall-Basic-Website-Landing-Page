Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Telecall Laguna?

Telecall Laguna is an accredited institution that offers training courses in Technical and Vocational Education (TVET) under the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED).

2. What types of courses or trainings does Telecall Laguna offer?

Telecall Laguna offers courses in various fields, including Creative Web Design, Event Management, Contact Center Services, and Application Development Management.

3. Are the courses at Telecall Laguna free?

Yes, Telecall Laguna provides free training courses in Creative Web Design, Event Management, Contact Center Services, and Application Development Management.

4. How can I enroll in a course at Telecall Laguna?

To enroll in a course, you can visit the Telecall website, select your desired course, and fill out the registration form with your information. It's that simple!

5. Is Telecall Laguna accredited by the relevant authorities?

Yes, Telecall Laguna is accredited by TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority) and CHED (Commission on Higher Education), ensuring the quality and legitimacy of its courses.

6. What sets Telecall Laguna apart from other educational institutions?

Telecall Laguna stands out for its commitment to providing free training courses in high-demand fields, making education accessible to a wider audience. The institution also boasts experienced instructors and a supportive learning environment.

7. Can I choose my preferred schedule for classes?

Yes, Telecall Laguna offers flexibility in scheduling to accommodate students' needs. You can select a schedule that suits your availability.

8. What career opportunities can I pursue after completing a course at Telecall Laguna?

Telecall Laguna's courses are designed to prepare students for various careers. After completing a course, you may pursue opportunities in web design, event planning, customer service, or application development, depending on your chosen program.

9. Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in Telecall Laguna's courses?

Prerequisites may vary depending on the course. Some programs may have specific requirements, while others may be open to individuals with a general interest in the field. Please check the course details for specific prerequisites.

10. How can I contact Telecall Laguna for more information or assistance?

For any inquiries or assistance, you can reach out to Telecall Laguna's support team through the contact information provided on this website. They are available to answer your questions and guide you through the enrollment process.
